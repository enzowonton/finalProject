The Journey of a Raindrop

As the raindrop descends from the sky, it approaches a bustling city below. It falls past towering skyscrapers and busy streets, witnessing the vibrant pulse of urban life. Amidst the hum of the city, the raindrop sees both the marvels of human creation and the challenges of pollution and waste. This part of its journey highlights the stark contrast between the natural world and the man-made one, reminding us of our profound impact on the environment.
